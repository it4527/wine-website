Featured Wines

Кaberne Wine

The grapes are picked manually in cassettes and carefully selected. Before processing, the grapes are refrigerated for about 20 hours. This is followed by sorting by sorting lines, ronching, re-sorting and crushing. Alcoholic fermentation is carried out in French oak barrels of 400l in 70% of the batch, and in the remaining 30% of the alcoholic fermentation is carried out in stainless steel fermenters of 10m³ and 15m³.

Featured Wines

Chardonnay Wine

The grapes are picked manually in cassettes and carefully selected. It is processed according to the classical technology, which includes the temperature of the grapes in a refrigerator for 20h to a temperature of 10-12˚C, sorting, ronching, crushing, infusing, draining and pressing. This is followed by clarification of the must and alcoholic fermentation at a temperature of 15-19 ° C. This scheme involves alcoholic fermentation and maturation in French oak barrels.

Merlo Wine

The wine is characterized by an intense red color with shades of purple. Concentrated and harmonious aroma of ripe red fruits, spices and subtle nuances of French oak. The taste of the wine is voluminous, with rounded tannins, juiciness and pronounced flavor. The all-natural character of the wine is complemented by the long and memorable finish. It goes very well with red meats, pasta, hot appetizers and chicken. The appropriate temperature for consumption is 18⁰-20⁰C
